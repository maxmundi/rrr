About Us

Letter from the owner.

Hi,

I'm Max, owner of Rapid Rain Response and a tech entrepreneur turned water whisperer.

Back in 2022 I bought an old house in the Mt. Washington area of Los Angeles which turned out not to be ready for the record rainfall we had that year.

I had leaks, my neighbors had leaks, my grocery store had leaks... leaks were everywhere I went. But because all of L.A. was battling the rain, contractors were slammed. I ended up spending a lot of time myself helping neighbors, climbing on roofs, finding leaks, and stopping the rain from getting in. It occured to me this is a service people really need - a rapid response to rain leaks that bridges the gap until a contractor can arrive with a permanent solution.

Now, Rapid Rain Response's specialized services, equipment, and skills even have veteran contractors calling us to diagnose water intrusion. Turns out that millions of dollars are wasted every year on the wrong solutions because contractors don't typically bother diagnosing problems. They'll rip out big sections of roof or recommend redoing whole walls because somewhere in there is a pinhole leak. With us, you fix only what needs fixing. To each problem a proportionate solution - to a pinhole leak a pinhole-sized fix.

Call us to protect your home better and help you save money doing it.

- Max Craig
