About Us

Letter from the owner.

Hi,

I'm Max, owner of Rapid Rain Response and tech manager turned water whisperer.

Back in 2022 I bought an old house in the Mt. Washington area of Los Angeles, and that same year our weather stretched the definition of "desert". My home and its flat roof were not ready for that record rainfall.

I had leaks, my neighbors had leaks, my grocery store had leaks... everywhere I went - leaks. But because all of L.A. was battling the rain, roofers were slammed. I ended up spending a lot of time myself helping neighbors, climbing up on roofs, finding leaks, stopping the rain from getting in. It occured to me that this is a service people could use - a rapid response to rain that bridges the gap until a roofer can show up.

Fast forward a year and Rapid Rain Response's specialized services, equipment, and skills have even roofers calling us to help them diagnose water intrusion. Turns out water is sneaky - gets in through the most unpredictable vulnerabilities. Also turns out we're really good at pinpointing those vulnerabilities.

What I hadn't anticipated is how much money we'd save clients. Roofers aren't diagnostic experts and don't have incentive to do the least work possible. They'll rip out big sections of roof because somewhere in there is a pinhole leak, then send you a big bill. With us, you fix only what needs fixing. To each problem a proportionate solution - to a pinhole leak a pinhole-sized fix.

Our weather is changing. We're here to help you adapt.

- Max Craig
